Our First Date

May 29th, 2021

It was our first date and I was so so nervous. You came to pick me up and funny enough, my dad was there with Sophie bidding me goodbye for the night. I remember sitting in your car and immediately getting a feeling of warmth around me. I felt like I had known you for centuries. We talked as we got to the movies and sat down in our seats. We were talking about our colleges and how we are so anxious for them to start. We watched through Spiral and when I closed my eyes, you were there to comfort me. As we finished up, we went back to the car and cuddled for a bit. Then you kissed me!!! I was in absolute shock because I had never kissed anyone before. I was so anxious because I thought I was going to fuck it up but no. You made me comfortable in my own skin and that will be something I will remember for the rest of my life. Finally, we had Chick-Fil-A and I had felt comfortable talking about my past. Right there, you made me feel a type of way that I have never felt before.
